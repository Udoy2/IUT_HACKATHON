# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Office staff/bosses monitoring office electrical usage

## Product Purpose

Real-time electrical monitoring for office devices with live dashboard and Discord bot to give the boss a clear, live picture of everything happening in the office at a glance, including visual representation of device states (e.g., lights glowing when ON, fans animating when running).

## Brand Personality

Friendly, approachable, warm

## Anti-references

Avoid cold, clinical, hospital-like dashboards; overly playful or toy-like interfaces; corporate dashboard with excessive charts and graphs.

## Design Principles

- Clarity and at-a-glance understanding: Users should instantly comprehend office status.
- Visual feedback: Device states should be represented intuitively (e.g., glowing lights, spinning fans).
- Friendly and approachable aesthetic: Design should feel welcoming, not cold or overly technical.
- Real-time responsiveness: Updates should be immediate to reflect live device status.
- Accessibility first: Ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for inclusive access.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, including considerations for reduced motion, color blindness, and screen reader usability.
